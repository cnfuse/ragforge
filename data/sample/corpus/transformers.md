# Transformers

The transformer is a neural-network architecture introduced in the 2017 paper
"Attention Is All You Need". It relies on a self-attention mechanism to weigh the
importance of different tokens in a sequence, replacing the recurrence used by
earlier models.

Transformers underpin modern large language models. Their ability to process
sequences in parallel and capture long-range dependencies made them the
foundation for systems used in translation, summarisation, and conversational AI.
